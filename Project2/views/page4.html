FAQ

Navigate to the "Recipes" section of the app. Click on the "Add New Recipe" button. Fill in the required details, such as the recipe name, ingredients, instructions, and any additional notes. Optionally, you can upload a photo of the dish. Once you have entered all the necessary information, click the "Save Recipe" button. Your new recipe will now be added to your collection and can be accessed anytime.

Navigate to the "Recipes" section of the app. Locate the recipe you want to edit or delete. Click on the recipe to open its detailed view. To edit the recipe, click on the "Edit Recipe" button. Make the necessary changes and click "Save" to update the recipe. To delete the recipe, click on the "Delete Recipe" button. Confirm the deletion when prompted. The recipe will be updated or removed from your collection accordingly.

To switch between light and dark themes in the Recipe Manager app, follow these steps: Locate the theme switcher button, which is usually found in the app's navigation bar or settings menu. Click on the theme switcher button. The icon may change to indicate the current theme (e.g., a sun icon for light theme and a moon icon for dark theme). The app will instantly switch to the selected theme, providing a more comfortable viewing experience based on your preference. You can toggle between the light and dark themes as needed to suit your environment and reduce eye strain.
